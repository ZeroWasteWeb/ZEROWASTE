Our Mission: Zero Waste, Zero Hunger

The Harvest Hub Vision

Our vision supports the mission of Zero waste, Zero hunger by making sure edible food never goes to waste and reaches people who need it most. We work to create a sustainable system where surplus food from farms, retailers, and restaurants is redirected to local communities instead of ending up in landfills.

Pillars of Our Work

  • Recovering surplus and near-expiry food to reduce waste while providing meals for families in need.
  • Helping businesses understand and reduce their food waste, supporting a cleaner and more responsible food ecosystem.
  • Working with volunteers, food banks, and shelters to distribute rescued food fairly and efficiently.

Every kilogram of food saved is a step toward a sustainable future and a meal for a hungry neighbor.
